How to Use This App
1
Upload Your Audio or Video File

Drag and drop your file or click to browse. Supported formats include MP3, MP4, WAV, AVI, MOV, FLAC, OGG, M4A, and WebM. Maximum file size is 200MB.

2
Choose Processing Options

Select a model size based on your needs (larger models are more accurate but slower). Optionally specify a language for better accuracy or let the app auto-detect it.

Choose between:

  • Transcribe: Generate subtitles in the original language
  • Translate: Convert the speech to English subtitles
3
Download Your Subtitles

Once processing is complete, you can preview the transcription and download your subtitles in one of these formats:

  • SRT: Standard subtitle format compatible with most video players
  • WebVTT: For web-based videos and HTML5 players
  • Plain Text: Simple text transcript without timestamps
Tips:
  • For best accuracy, use clear audio with minimal background noise
  • Larger models provide better results but take longer to process
  • Specifying the correct language usually improves transcription quality
